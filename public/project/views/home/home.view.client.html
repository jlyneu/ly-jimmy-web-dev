What is PetShelter?
PetShelter is a platform for people to find pets to adopt from local animal shelters. Animal shelter owners are also able to list their shelter and pets for adoption.
What kind of paperwork do I need to fill out?
Paperwork will vary for each shelter. You won't actually be adopting pets through PetShelter, but PetShelter provides means of finding the perfect pet and contacting the shelter to get the adoption process started.
How much does PetShelter cost?
PetShelter is a free service! Simply click the button below to start a quick search in your area. Once you find the pet that you've been looking for, simply reach out to the provided contact and you'll be bringing your new buddy home in no time!
